The Area Model

The Area Model is a way to break down multiplication into a series of smaller steps.

To solve single digit problems, look at the problem as a measurement of length multiplied by height.

To solve two digit problems, write the numbers in expanded form as tens place and ones place. Then match each of these four numbers to one side of a four-box grid. Find the area of each box and multiply them together to get the total area.
